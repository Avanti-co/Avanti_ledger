The Guardian Angel for Your Numbers

Human oversights shouldn't compromise your livelihood. Avanti contains a native Error Detection Engine built directly into the tracking pipeline to secure historical integrity.

If today's starting inventory conflicts with yesterday's closing parameters, or if a closing item magically exceeds your total stock, the platform alerts you right away. It even prevents accidental profit losses by alerting you if a form-entered selling price dips under cost constraints.

To secure absolute accuracy across time, our Automatic Item Propagation engine carries structural name/price changes exclusively to future days, protecting the sanctity of verified past data.
